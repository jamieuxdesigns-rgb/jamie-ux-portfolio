Project Overview

Designed a responsive website for Woof and Wiggles, a dog care company, focusing on easy booking, clear service information, and engaging visuals to delight pet owners.

Design Approach

Problem

The original Camp Woofs and Wiggles website presented challenges for users: navigation was unclear, scheduling training sessions or daycare visits was difficult to locate, and information about services was dense, creating friction for dog owners seeking quick answers.

Solution

A user-centered redesign introduced streamlined navigation, a clear and prominent booking flow, improved content hierarchy, and a friendly, inviting visual style that reflects the warmth and care of the camp.

Outcome

The final prototype demonstrates faster task completion, stronger visual trust signals, and a responsive layout that ensures a seamless, intuitive experience for modern pet owners looking to book daycare or training services.

UX Process

🔍

Research

Reviewed dog owners’ needs, evaluated the original site structure, and identified friction points in navigation and booking daycare or training sessions

✏️

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity layouts to organize service information, simplify navigation, and prioritize scheduling actions.

🧩

Prototyping

Designed responsive high-fidelity screens in Figma with accessible typography, warm visuals, and clear user flows.

✅

Evaluation

Tested usability and clarity of core tasks to ensure the redesigned experience supports real-world pet owners booking services.
