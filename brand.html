Future Funds App

Redesigning a financial platform to make investment tracking simple and intuitive for everyday users.

View Interactive Prototype

Project Overview

FutureFunds was struggling with low user engagement due to a cluttered dashboard and confusing navigation. Target users were millennials and Gen Z investors who needed quick, clear insights on their portfolios. With a 3-week sprint and limited development resources, I led the redesign process, creating wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and an interactive prototype to improve usability and engagement.
