Horizon Railways Ticketing Experience

Designing a faster, clearer mobile ticket purchase flow for everyday travelers.

Project Overview

Horizon Railways is a conceptual mobile ticketing experience focused on helping commuters quickly find routes, compare ticket options, and complete purchases with minimal friction. The project centered on simplifying navigation and reducing the time required to buy a ticket on mobile devices.

Problem

Many transportation apps require too many steps to search routes, compare prices, and complete checkout. Confusing layouts and dense information slow commuters who are often in a hurry.

Design Challenge: How might we streamline the mobile ticket purchase experience so users can confidently buy a ticket in seconds?
