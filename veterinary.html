Project Overview

This conceptual UX/UI redesign reimagines the online experience for a local veterinary clinic. The goal was to reduce friction in appointment scheduling, organize service information more clearly, and establish a friendly visual tone that builds trust with pet owners.

Using a user-centered design process, the project moved from research and structure planning to wireframing, high-fidelity prototyping, and responsive layout refinement. The final solution demonstrates how thoughtful interaction design and clear information hierarchy can meaningfully improve usability in healthcare-adjacent digital spaces.

Design Approach

Problem

The original Purrfectly Peppy website presented challenges for pet owners: navigation was confusing, scheduling appointments was not immediately obvious, and information about services was dense and difficult to scan. Users struggled to find quick answers for their pets’ care needs.

Solution

A user-centered redesign introduced streamlined navigation, a clear and prominent appointment booking flow, better-organized content hierarchy, and a friendly, professional visual style that conveys trust and approachability.

Outcome

The final prototype demonstrates more efficient task completion, stronger visual trust cues, and a responsive layout that adapts seamlessly across devices, ensuring an intuitive experience for modern veterinary clients.

UX Process

🔍

Research

Reviewed user needs, analyzed the original site structure, and identified friction points in navigation and appointment booking. Developed low-fidelity wireframes to reorganize content, simplify navigation, and emphasize the scheduling process.

✏️

Wireframing

Created responsive high-fidelity designs in Figma with accessible typography, calm and welcoming visuals, and clear, logical user flows. Evaluated usability to ensure core tasks—like booking an appointment or finding service information—are smooth and straightforward.

🧩

Prototyping

Created responsive high-fidelity designs in Figma featuring accessible typography, inviting visuals, and intuitive user flows.

✅

Evaluation

Conducted usability reviews to ensure core tasks, such as appointment booking and service exploration, are clear, efficient, and support the needs of real-world veterinary clients.

Research & Design

The original design suffered from clutter, weak visual hierarchy, and limited accessibility cues. The redesign employs thoughtful color-coding, icons, and structured layouts to improve readability and usability. Prioritizing clear navigation and easy appointment scheduling significantly enhances the user experience.

Warm imagery, clean typography, and generous spacing make the veterinary services feel approachable and reliable, while the structured UX process—from research to prototype—delivers meaningful improvements that go beyond surface-level design.
