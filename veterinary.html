A user-centered website redesign for a veterinary clinic focused on simplifying appointment scheduling, improving content clarity, and creating a warm, trustworthy digital experience for pet owners.

View Case Study

Project Overview

This conceptual UX/UI redesign reimagines the online experience for a local veterinary clinic. The goal was to reduce friction in appointment scheduling, organize service information more clearly, and establish a friendly visual tone that builds trust with pet owners.

Using a user-centered design process, the project moved from research and structure planning to wireframing, high-fidelity prototyping, and responsive layout refinement. The final solution demonstrates how thoughtful interaction design and clear information hierarchy can meaningfully improve usability in healthcare-adjacent digital spaces.

Design Approach

Problem

The original veterinary website lacked clear navigation, made appointment booking difficult to find, and presented dense service information that created friction for pet owners seeking quick answers.

Solution

A user-centered redesign introduced simplified navigation, a prominent scheduling flow, improved content hierarchy, and a warm visual style to create a more approachable and intuitive digital experience.

Outcome

The final prototype demonstrates clearer task completion, stronger visual trust signals, and a responsive layout that supports real-world usability for modern veterinary clients.
