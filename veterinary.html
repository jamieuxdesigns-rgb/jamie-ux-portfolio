Veterinary Website

Designing a calm, trust-building digital experience for pet owners.

View Project Details

Project Overview

Role: UX/UI Designer

Timeline: 3 weeks

Tools: Figma, wireframes, prototyping

This project focused on designing a pet care website that feels welcoming and trustworthy, with intuitive navigation and calming visual design to help pet owners easily access veterinary services and information.

Design Process

1. Wireframes & Sitemap

Created clear navigation and user flows for services, appointments, and resources.

2. Visual Design

Applied calming color palette, readable typography, and consistent branding.

3. Prototyping

Developed clickable Figma prototype to test user navigation and content hierarchy.

Outcome & Impact

  • Improved clarity and navigation for pet owners.
  • Reduced anxiety through calming colors and visual cues.
  • Created a trustworthy digital presence for veterinary services.
